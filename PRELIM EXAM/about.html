Who We Are

The Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS) is a recognized student organization that brings together students who are passionate about computing, information technology, and innovation. It serves as a platform for learning, collaboration, and leadership development beyond the classroom.

To enhance studentsâ€™ technical skills, leadership abilities, and professionalism through seminars, workshops, competitions, and community involvement.

Vision

To be a leading student organization that produces competent, innovative, and socially responsible IT professionals who can contribute to national development.

Core Values

JPCS upholds excellence, integrity, teamwork, innovation, and continuous learning in all its programs and activities.

Why Join JPCS?

Being a member of JPCS allows students to expand their knowledge, gain hands-on experience, build professional networks, and develop leadership skills that are essential in the IT industry.

Members actively participate in events that prepare them for real-world challenges, making JPCS an important part of their academic and professional journey.
