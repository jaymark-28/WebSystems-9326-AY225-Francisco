JPCS Events & Activities

Discover seminars, workshops, competitions, and activities designed to enhance students’ skills and experience.

Events Overview

The Junior Philippine Computer Society organizes various academic and non-academic events that aim to develop students’ technical knowledge, leadership abilities, and teamwork skills.

Through these activities, members are exposed to real-world scenarios that prepare them for future careers in the IT industry.

Event Categories

Seminars

Educational talks conducted by professionals and experts that focus on current trends, technologies, and best practices in the field of Information Technology.

Workshops

Hands-on training sessions where students gain practical experience by working on real tasks, tools, and technologies.

Competitions

Competitive activities that challenge students’ problem-solving skills, creativity, and teamwork while encouraging innovation.
